begin again

Thu, Aug 18, 2016

I’m not much for revisiting the past. I suppose this is both a good thing and a bad thing but for whatever reason I much prefer looking down the road ahead. At the very least I avoid a sore neck by rarely looking over my shoulder.

So after a couple years of silence I decided to nuke several years of content and start fresh. No excess baggage. No maintenance, just a clean slate.

I’m also somewhat averse to following plans. I’d rather follow whims. And so, as before, it is difficult to say what will end up here. I would imagine there will be some tech (I’ve been in IT, primarily as a software developer, for over 30 years), some music (I’ve been playing some instrument or another since I was 5 and studied music in college), and some… well, meandering.

And so it begins.
